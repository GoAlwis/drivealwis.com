Eco Friendly Products

The seas are filling up with plastics. We have come up with innovative and eco-friendly solution- Recycling Plastic waste materials help to reduce the strain on the finite resources of Earth like Natural gas, Petroleum, Coal, Wood and Water. By recycling plastic instead of manufacturing the same grade of material every time, we are effectively reducing the footprint of plastic on dump sites across the globe and Oceans are not the best dump site for plastic waste. We will try to lower the energy consumption per one vehicle produced and we look that 995 of annual production waste is recycled and used. The ALWIS group aims to reduce supply chain CO2 emission by atleast 30%-40% by 2030. For us Sustainability is more than a passing fad. We accept responsibility-now and in the future and will take sustainability to a whole new level.
